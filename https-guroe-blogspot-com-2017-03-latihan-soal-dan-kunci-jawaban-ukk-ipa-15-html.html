Soal 1:

Choose the correct form of the verb to complete the sentence:

My sister ________ tennis every Saturday morning.

a) plays

b) play

c) played

d) playing

Jawaban: a) plays

Soal 2:

Complete the sentence with the correct preposition:

I'm going to the store ________ some groceries.

a) at

b) with

c) for

d) to

Jawaban: c) for

Soal 3:

Choose the correct question tag for the following sentence:

He doesn't like coffee, ________?

a) does he?

b) isn't he?

c) doesn't he?

d) don't he?

Jawaban: a) does he?

Soal 4:

Identify the correct comparative form of the adjective "good":

a) gooder

b) goodest

c) better

d) goodier

Jawaban: c) better

Soal 5:

Choose the correct form of the verb to complete the sentence:

She ________ a book when I arrived.

a) reads

b) is reading

c) read

d) has read

Jawaban: b) is reading

Soal 6:

Choose the correct word order for the sentence:

carrots / do / like / you / ?

a) You do like carrots?

b) Do you like carrots?

c) Carrots you do like?

d) Do like you carrots?

Jawaban: b) Do you like carrots?

Soal 7:

Identify the correct spelling of the word:

a) beutiful

b) beutifull

c) beautiful

d) beautifull

Jawaban: c) beautiful

Soal 8:

Choose the correct form of the verb to complete the sentence:

The students ________ to the teacher's instructions.

a) listens

b) listen

c) listened

d) listening

Jawaban: b) listen

Soal 9:

Choose the correct possessive form of the noun:

The dog is ________ bone.

a) its

b) it's

c) their

d) they're

Jawaban: a) its

Soal 10:

Identify the correct punctuation for the sentence:

My favorite color is blue ________.

a) .

b) ,

c) !

d) ?

Jawaban: a) .

B. Jawaban dan Pembahasan

1. Diketahui:

Balok dengan ukuran:

Panjang (p) = 12cm =0,12m

Lebar (l) = 8cm = 0,8cm

Tinggi (t) = 3cm = 0,03m

Gaya berat (w) sebesar 30 N

Ditanya P=?............

Dijawab=

a. Luas Permukaan Aa= Panjang(p) x tinggi (t) = 0,12x0,03 = 0,0036 m2

Tekanan yang dihasilkan balok pada posisi A ;

Pa = F/Aa = 30N/0,0036m2 = 8333,3N/m2

b. Luas permukaan Ab = lebar (l) x tinggi (t) = 0,08x0,03= 0,0024m2

Tekanan yang dihasilkan balok pada posisi B :

Pb = F/Ab = 30N/0,0024m2 = 12.500N/m2

c. Luas permukaan (Ac) = Panjang (p) x lebar (l) = 0,12 x 0,08 = 0,0096m2

Tekanan yang dihasilkan balok pada posisi C :

Pc = F/Ac = 30 N/0,0096m2 = 3.125n/M2

2. Diketahui:

Massa benda (m) = 1 ton = 1000 kg

Maka, gaya berat (W) = m x g =1000kg x 9,8m/s2 = 9.800 N

Gaya berat dapat diasumsikan sebagai F2Luas penampang piston besar (A2) = 0,2m2

Luas penampang piston kecil (A1) = 0,02m2

Ditanya : F1………..?

Pada pompa hidrolik berlaku hokum Pascal :

F1/A1 = F2/A2 = F1A2 =F2A1

F1 = F2A1/A2

F1 = 9.800 X 0,02/0,2

F1 = 980 N

Jadi, gaya minimal (F1) yang diperlukan untuk mengangkat benda sebesar 980N
