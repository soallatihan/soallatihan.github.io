17. He will be late for school if he does not leave at once. (Jawaban A)

18. If we had woken up earlier, we would have reached school. (Jawaban D)

19. What is the meaning of the sentence?

Jawaban A. Love can be represent by music

20. What is the aim of the text?

Jawaban D. To inform that music can bring love

21. What is advantage of the text?

Jawaban E. To inform that music can show a love

22. Why does father in the picture play bamboo flute?

Because, bamboo flute represent not simple, warm, and modern. (Jawaban E)

PEMBAHASAN

(17) Conditional Sentence Type 1

Simple Future ( IF ) Simple Present (+) S + will be + complement (IF) S + does not + V1

Jadi, jawaban A karena does not + V1 = leave.

(18) Conditional Sentence Type 2

IF + Past Perfect + Past Future Perfect IF (S + had + V3) (S + would have + V3)

Jadi, jawaban D karena had + V3 = woken up.

Teks untuk No. 19-22 : The Music Of Love

(19) Arti secara bahasa adalah music cinta yang berarti cinta bisa diwakilkan melalui music. Jadi, jawaban A.(20) Tujuan teks itu untuk memberitahu pembaca bahwa musik bisa membawa cinta. Jadi. jawaban D (21) Manfaat dari teks itu adalah bahwa dengan musik kita bisa menunjukkan cinta kita. Jadi, jawaban E.(22) Alasan sang ayah menggunakan seruling bamby karena menunjukkan kesederhanaan dan kehangatan sebab cinta tidak harus mahal. Jadi, jawaban E.
