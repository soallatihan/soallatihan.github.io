Global warming is a serious environmental issue. It refers to the increase in the Earth's average surface temperature due to human activities, such as the burning of fossil fuels and deforestation. - B. The rise in Earth's average surface temperature due to human activities

- A. Planting more trees

- B. Burning fossil fuels and deforestation

- C. Decreasing sea levels

- D. Cold weather patterns
